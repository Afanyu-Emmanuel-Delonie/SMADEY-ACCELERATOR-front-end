About Us

SMADEY

Smadey Accelerator, a beacon of hope and opportunity in the world of finance. Founded on March 27, 2024, by a dynamic group of young visionaries who make up the SMADEY group from the Northwest region of Cameroon, Smadey Accelerator is more than just a fintech platform – it's a movement. Driven by the desire to empower individuals and communities, we embarked on a journey to redefine the way people think about money. Inspired by the rich cultural heritage and entrepreneurial spirit of our homeland, we set out to create a platform that transcends boundaries and empowers dreams. At SMADEY, we believe that financial freedom is not just a distant goal it's a fundamental right.

Our Mission

We envision a world where everyone has the opportunity to build a secure financial future, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through innovative solutions and unwavering dedication, we strive to break down barriers and pave the way towards economic empowerment for all.
